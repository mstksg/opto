runOptoParallel :: (MonadUnliftIO m, Fractional a) => RunOpts m a -> ParallelOpts -> a -> OptoM m v a -> m (a, [OptoM m v a]) Source #

Optimize an a times in parallel, and aggregate all the results together by taking the mean. Freezes and returns all of the updated optimizer states from each thread.
