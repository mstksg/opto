class Monad m => Ref m a v | v -> a where Source #

Abstraction over mutable references of types. A Ref m a v means that a v is a mutable reference to an a, and we may updatewritemodify v in context m.

This allows us to reat mutable vectors and in-place mutable numbers or records in the same way.

Minimal complete definition
